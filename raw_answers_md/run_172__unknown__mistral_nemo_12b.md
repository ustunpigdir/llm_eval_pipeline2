   \[
   c/H_0 = \frac{3 \times 10^5 \text{ km/s}}{70.0 \text{ km/s/Mpc}} \approx 4315.79 \text{ Mpc}
   \]
   The linear term \(cz/H_0\) at \(z = 0.200\) is:
   \[
   cz/H_0 = \frac{(0.200 \times 3 \times 10^5 \text{ km/s})}{70.0 \text{ km/s/Mpc}} \approx 863.16 \text{ Mpc}
   \]

   \[

   \[
